Great Auntie Thelma's Shortbread

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all purpose flower
  • 1/2 cup corn starch
  • 1 cup icing sugar
  • 1 pound butter (Or half butter and half margarine)

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 325Â°F
  • Cream together the butter and sugar
  • Mix together the flower and corn starch. Add to butter and sugar.
  • Blend with mixer until well mixed.
  • Drop by the teaspoon onto an ungreased cookie sheet. (Optionally covered with parchment paper)
  • Bake in oven for 15-17 minutes on the middle rack.
  • Wash baking sheet between batches.
  • Makes 6-7 dozen.
