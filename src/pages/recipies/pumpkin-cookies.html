Pumpkin Cookies

Summary

Simple pumpkin cookies. Makes enough for and goes well with Pennche Frosting

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
  • 1 cup margarine or butter, softened
  • 1 cup canned or fresh pumpkin
  • 1 tso. vanilla
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups all purpose baking flower
  • 1 tsp. baking powder
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1 tsp. cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 3/4 cup chopped walnuts or peacans, optional

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 350F
  2. In a large bowl beat sugar and margarine until light and fluffy
  3. Add pumpkin, vanilla, and egg. Beat well.
  4. Add flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Stir in walnuts or pecans.
  6. Drop by rounded teaspoonfuls two inches appart onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  7. Bake at 350F for 10-12 minutes or until light golden brown around edges.
  8. Immediately remove from cookie sheets, cool completely.
  9. Optionally add frosting.
