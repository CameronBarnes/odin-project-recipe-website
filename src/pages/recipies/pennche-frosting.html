Pennche Frosting

Summary

Goes well with and makes enough frosting for one batch of Pumpkin Cookies

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp. margarine or butter.
  • 1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 - 2 cups powdered sugar

Instructions

  1. In medium saucepan, combine margarine and brown sugar.
  2. Bring to a boil, cook over medium heat for one minute or until slightly thickened, stiring constantly.
  3. Cool ten minutes.
  4. Add milk, beat until smooth.
  5. Beat in enough powdered sugar for desired spreading consistency.
  6. Frost cooled cookies.
  7. Let stand until frosting is set.
